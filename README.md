**Practice 10 – Pygame Projects**
This folder has three games made with Python and Pygame: Paint, Racer, and Snake.

Racer needs an images folder in the same place as racer.py with these files: way.png, car.png, enemy_car.png, coin.png, game_over.jpeg.
Paint
A drawing program. Use the buttons on the left to pick a color, draw circles or rectangles, erase, or clear the canvas.
Racer
Drive your car with arrow keys, collect coins to get points, and avoid enemy cars. Every 5 coins the level goes up and enemies get faster. Score and level are shown in the top right corner.
Snake
Move the snake with arrow keys and eat the red food. Every 3 points the level goes up and the snake gets faster. The game ends if the snake hits a wall or itself. Press Space to restart or Escape to quit.
